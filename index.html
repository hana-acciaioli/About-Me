Hana from The Dalles

Hello, my name is Hana. I live in The Dalles, Oregon.
I moved to The Dalles, AKA The Dirty D, two years ago. Ask a local why it's called this and you'll get a different answer every time.
Three years ago, I NEVER thought I would live here, but here I am. And I kind of like it.

A few cool things about The Dalles:

  • We have THE BEST pizza.
  • We are located perfectly between Portland, Smith Rock State Park, and Tieton River Gorge.
  • There are so many wild flowers in the spring!
